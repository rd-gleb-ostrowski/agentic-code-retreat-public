How the morning starts

The Marketplace

Pick what you'll work on, then find your people.

🧩 A Menu Project 🚀 The Arena 💡 Your own idea

Teams of ~1–4. Same project, brand-new codebase.

Walk through the three tracks next. Then open the floor for participants' own pitches, then we group up.

The Menu 🧩

Five small projects. Each starts simple and never runs out of road.

Every one ships with a clear spec, a ladder of expansions… and a few surprises the facilitators are keeping to themselves 😈

One slide each. Pitch the hook, hint at the depth. Don't reveal curveballs.
algorithmic · visual

Game of Life, pimped

Conway's classic cellular automaton — then make it unmistakably yours.

Starts: a wrapping grid that comes alive · grows into: your own rules, a hex world, an endless zoomable universe, a real GUI.

TUI · games

Retro Arcade

A whole arcade that lives in your terminal.

Starts: a launcher + Snake, with bling & sound · grows into: Tetris, Space Invaders, Hunt the Wumpus, high scores, two-player.

web / GUI · absurdist

The Calculator That Refuses

A calculator with… opinions. It computes correctly — when it feels like it.

Starts: real maths, but it refuses cursed numbers, division by 13, déjà vu · grows into: moods, negotiation, advanced calculator features

fantasy · meatier

Arcana

Magic isn't code — it's verse. Cast spells as rhyming couplets.

Starts: a couplet that rhymes & scans strikes the golem · grows into: a fuller lexicon, duels, haiku, multiplayer.

The meatiest of the five — great for someone who wants a deeper challenge.
sci-fi · systems sim

Starship Bridge

Never enough power, and the universe keeps trying to kill you. Keep her flying.

Starts: route scarce reactor power across five systems as crises hit · grows into: repair crews, a campaign, a whole fleet.

The competition

🚀 The Arena

Shatterbelt Salvagers

Salvage ships drift the debris of a shattered world, scavenging relics and fighting over them.

This is the head-to-head track. The fun bit: you don't play — you write the bot that plays.

You program the bot ⚔️

  • Pilot a ship: thrust, shoot, grab relics, bank them
  • Connect live over WebSocket, or upload a WASM bot
  • Matches run on the big screen — most relics banked wins
Be honest that the bespoke arena may not be fully ready & contain 🐛s.

Your turn

Brought your own quest? 💡

Pitch it — 30 seconds, on your feet.

  • What is it, and why is it fun?
  • How many hands do you need?

Mentoring others on your project counts double. 🙌

Open the floor. Keep pitches snappy. Jot project names + how many people each wants on a whiteboard.

Let's group up

Find your crew 🤝

  1. Pick a project that excites you
  2. Cluster into teams of 1–4
  3. Grab a table, name your team, say hi
  4. Point your agent at the spec — and go 🏁
Help solo/floating people find a group. Nudge balanced team sizes. Then kick off the morning session.

Have fun 🚀
